FULL CASE STUDY For LOCA-GO, a scavenger hunt app

On my mission to providing access to relevant and upscale information for discovering cities and their surroundings, I developed LOCA-GO. It’s an easy and fun way to have a plethora of different tour guides at hand and select a matching one with just a few taps. With LOCA-GO, people are now able to undertake tours matching their personal preferences regarding topics, time and scope.
