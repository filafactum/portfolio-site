About

My Story

I caught fire.

I always thought my dreamjob would be to create highly individual, embroidered products with thoughtful details for my customers to make the celebration of their births, baptisms and weddings extra-special. I already had more than a decade of experience in conceptualising and ideating for my own business and external art projects. But there was something missing, I wanted more. And then I cought fire: on the lookout for a new programm to manage my taxes and income I ran into one that was not only very easy to use but was really fun to use. I asked myself how it could be possible to make such a boring subject this simple and fun to use? During research I came across the principles of the "User Centered Design Process". And I knew: I need to learn this!

Since then I not only came to mastering all skills needed for the developing and design of user-friendly digital products: Information architecture, wireframing and user testing, to name just a few. To fully understand what's needed for the setup of a digital product and what's actually possible, I got my feet wet on the basics in frontend-development. I'm talking HTML, CSS and JavaScript here. Because only from understanding technics and applying user-friendly design there can be a solution for the real problems of real people- doesn't that sound amazing? In the same way a software made my life easier, I am now contributing to a team to make a difference in peoples' lifes.
