My Story

Hi, my name is Annegret Kerschis. I am a certified UX designer with a background in art and design and excellent organisational skills. Working as a freelance textile designer and artist for many years, I gained experience in concepting, ideating and creating for my clients’ needs. These very same principles I apply now for implementing a better user experience in everyday technology to make digital products more simple and cheerful to use.

To unwind from digital work and keeping connected to the world, I enjoy putting my hands into my gardens’ ground or doing house projects There is (almost) no better treat than harvesting warm tomatoes and berries right from the plant!
